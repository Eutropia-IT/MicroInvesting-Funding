GOAL

Many youngsters who have promising startup ideas can raise funds for their startups.Our mission is creating more opportunities for new entrepreneurs to develop their startups. By mutual collaboration of the investors and entrepreneurs we will diminish unemployment from Bangladesh

HOW WE WORK

Investor

A registered user is not am investor until he/she invests any amount of money to any startup campaign. To invest funds, user has to deposit money to his/her account. The deposited amount will kept in his/her account as balance. After making any investment, the invested amount will be deducted from account balance and added to the invested balance. Before investing any amount, a user must read all the details of a project. Once an investment made, it cannot be undone until the threshold time completes. After investing funds to a project, users have to wait until the threshold time to generating revenue arrives. When the revenue generating threshold time arrives, users start to getting profits as per share percentage.

Entrepreuner

Any registered user can apply to raise funds for his/her startup idea. For applying one must switch to entrepreneur mode first, then he/she have to choose the 'Apply for fund' button and fill the required information (i.e. Detailed idea, finctionality, feasibility, revenue etc.) After submitting the idea, he/she has yo wait until our business analyst approves their ideas. Once approved, the emteepreneurs have to meet physically and present a pitch and disscuss about the pertnership with the investors. And they'll provide a threshold time for generating the revenue. After all these procedures, the idea will be live at user's dashboard with analyst ratings, where investors can invest their money. Once the necessary fund is raised, it'll be sent to the entreprenuer with an deed agreement.
